The following monsters are in the AdventureMaker database and may be refered to by name.

The following spells are in the AdventureMaker database and may be refered to by name when adding magic items to an adventure.

The following NPCs are in the AdventureMaker database. When refering to them with an NPC tag, use the tag entry show for each NPC. This is usually the NPCs name, but may not always be. For example, an NPC that changes over time may have different descriptions and different tags but the same name.

The following magic items are in the AdventureMaker database. When including them in an adventure, you must specify the fields listed in the needs column.

The following works are in the AdventureMaker master bibliography. You may refer to them with by citation tag by using the listed tag. When you do, the tag will expand to the name of the work. If the work is available for purchase, the name will be hyperlinke to the appropriate site and product. A full bibliographic entry will also appear at the end of the adventure.
